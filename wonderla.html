Wonderla:

If your desire for a thrill is unquenchable, then join the newest member of India's number 1 Amusement Park, Wonderla for memories of a lifetime. Visit Wonderla Hyderabad for an exhilarating experience that is bound to leave you spellbound. Brace yourself for a thrilling day, get your family and friends along for their 44 world-class rides that will mesmerize you and leave you asking for more. Join them to soak and splash in their exhilarating water rides, feel the rush of adrenaline on high-thrill rides, and get pampered with the finest facilities in leisure for the whole family. When you are tired with playing all the games, get to tingle your taste buds and energize yourself with their multi-cuisine restaurants.
